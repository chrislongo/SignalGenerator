# Privacy Policy

**SignalGenerator SG-2400**
*Last updated: April 4, 2026*

## Overview

SignalGenerator SG-2400 is an audio signal generator app developed by Chris Longo. Your privacy is important to me.

## Data Collection

This app does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No usage data or analytics are gathered
- No data is shared with third parties
- No cookies or tracking technologies are used
- No account creation is required
- No network connections are made

## Audio

The app generates audio tones locally on your device. All audio processing happens entirely on-device and no audio data is recorded or transmitted.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.
